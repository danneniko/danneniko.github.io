Lemonbite

Design & Development

A friend of mine who had a history as a salesperson wanted to start selling websites cold-calling.

He wanted a very simple and visually clean website with a brief information about the company. Smooth and subtle CSS3 animations, a simple grid and we were ready to go.

However, he found out that selling websites cold-calling wasn't the easiest task, since most clients actually require massive trust in their webdesign company before they even think about going into work. Using the phone as a sales weapon isn't the optimal way to go about sales in the design industry.

Despite that, this was probably the most important project for me as a freelancer. It allowed me to understand a whole lot about building trust with clients and how important it is to be extremely professional as a designer.

It was also this project that fueled my passion for web related work and business on the whole.
