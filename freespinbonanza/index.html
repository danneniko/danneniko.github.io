Freespin Bonanza is a community for online casino reviews.

The website had to have an old feel to it. The brand is aiming to relate to the old saloon gambling houses from the western era.

A website of this kind needs to build massive trust and comfort among the visitors in order to have any sort of impact. Hence the saloon-ish wooden menu, the wooden logo & the mood-setting background images.

My role was to design the wordpress theme, built upon html5blank, where the foundations of the core PHP is already done. Being mainly a designer and not a PHP-developer, I still managed to build this website according to the plan.

I used a masonry grid for the different reviews, and a very straightforward approach designwise. Where the visitors have full overview of the different offers.

On a type of website where the information easily can become overwhelming, I found it important to structure it in this manner.

Since over 35% of the visitors use mobile devices, I used media queries to optimize it for all devices. The information should be as easily accessible for mobile devices as it is for desktops.
