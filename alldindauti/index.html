I got the privilege to work with a very talented 3D Artist who has done work for respectable brands like Lego, Reebok, Gore-tex & more.

Alldin Dauti knew exactly what he wanted as for his website. He wanted a horizontal scrolling website where the content is delivered to the visitor at the center of the screen.

To further strenghten the congruence of being a 3D artist, he wanted the portfolio items to transform in a 3D manner when they are being viewed. The result can be seen here.

I have put emphasis on his work, which is the first thing visitors/clients want to see when they visit the portfolio of a VFX/3D artist.

It is also a very important aspect to be able to quickly see the next project without having to go back to the portfolio, hence the scrollable work content.
