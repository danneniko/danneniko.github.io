UsTwo

WP/AngularJS Development

Thanks to Additude Group, I got introduced to the international digital agency UsTwo. They have worked along with some of the most powerful brands in the world. They are also well known for their award-winning mobile game Monument Valley. So I guess it's needless to say that I felt very honored to work with them in the making of a promotion site for their banking client Sparbanken Syd.

Along with one of their inhouse visual designers, Jeremy Godefroid and one of their UX designers by the name Wayne Minton, we faced a lot of fun challenges and created a customer experience which was widely appreciated by the client in the end.

My role was to develop their new promotional landing page, a Wordpress site which the client could easily have full control over. And the signup form for the new campaign, a more advanced kind of form where I used Angular on the front end. It can be seen here

This journey was probably the most fun project I've worked on so far, and got to learn a great amount of new things when it comes to teamwork, deadline management, planning but also gained some new development knowledge.
