Mobile optimization

Lund is a city with a high turnover on new visitors. New students, businessmen from all over the world visiting the international corporations and even a few tourists.

The likely scenario in this case is that a lot of these visitors are apt to use their phones and tablets to guide themselves through the city.

I got in touch with Glorias, one of the most famous resturants in Lund. After a cup of coffee with one of the co-owners, there was no doubt about their willingness to to optimize the website for this target group.

With an initial sketch, I proposed which parts are the most important ones to cast light upon for a mobile visitor:

  • The opening hours should be easily spotted

  • It has to be very straightforward to navigate to the restaurant

  • One should easily be able to click and call the restaurant to book a table

  • The menu

Through a good collaboration with their graphical designer, the result was a successful solution which maintained the original company profile.
